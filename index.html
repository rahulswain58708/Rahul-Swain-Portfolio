01 — About

Building things I wish existed

Hello! I'm Rahul Swain, a Computer Science student, entrepreneur, and AI enthusiast from India. My goal is to build technology products that create real value for people and make advanced concepts easier to understand.

I'm passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Software Development, and Entrepreneurship. I believe in learning by building — which is why I spend my time creating projects, experimenting with new technologies, and sharing my journey publicly.

I founded MediaSpectAI, an AI-powered platform that helps detect AI-generated and manipulated media using multi-signal forensic analysis. I'm also the author of PySimplify, a beginner-friendly Python ebook designed to teach programming through simple explanations, practical examples, and mini-projects.

My long-term mission is to build impactful AI products, contribute to the developer community, and inspire students to learn technology through hands-on projects and continuous experimentation.
